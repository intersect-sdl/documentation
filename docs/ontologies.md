> **Disclaimer:** Some ontologies and namespaces listed here may be extended or customized per deployment.

SDL integrates and aligns multiple standard and custom ontologies to enable rich semantic modeling of scientific workflows, content, systems, and data. This document lists commonly used namespaces and selected key classes.

---

## Standard Ontologies

| Prefix | Namespace | Example Classes | SDL Descriptions |
|--------|-----------|------------------|------------------|
| `rdf:` | [rdf](http://www.w3.org/1999/02/22-rdf-syntax-ns#) | [`rdf:Property`](http://www.w3.org/1999/02/22-rdf-syntax-ns#Property), [`rdf:Statement`](http://www.w3.org/1999/02/22-rdf-syntax-ns#Statement) | N/A |
| `rdfs:` | [rdfs](http://www.w3.org/2000/01/rdf-schema#) | [`rdfs:Class`](http://www.w3.org/2000/01/rdf-schema#Class), [`rdfs:label`](http://www.w3.org/2000/01/rdf-schema#label) |  N/A |
| `owl:` | [owl](http://www.w3.org/2002/07/owl#) | [`owl:Class`](http://www.w3.org/2002/07/owl#Class), [`owl:Ontology`](http://www.w3.org/2002/07/owl#Ontology) |  N/A |
| `dcat:` | [dcat](http://www.w3.org/ns/dcat#) | [:rdfterm[dcat:Catalog]](http://www.w3.org/ns/dcat#Catalog), [:rdfterm[dcat:Dataset]](http://www.w3.org/ns/dcat#Dataset), [:rdfterm[dcat:Distribution]](http://www.w3.org/ns/dcat#Distribution) | [dcat:Dataset](/docs/entities/dcat_dataset) |
| `ssn:` | [ssn](http://www.w3.org/ns/ssn/) | [`ssn:Sensor`](http://www.w3.org/ns/ssn/Sensor), [:rdfterm[ssn:Deployment]](http://www.w3.org/ns/ssn/Deployment) | [ssn:Deployment](/docs/entities/ssn_deployment) |
| `sosa:` | [sosa](http://www.w3.org/ns/sosa/) | [:rdfterm[sosa:Observation]](http://www.w3.org/ns/sosa/Observation), [:rdfterm[sosa:Platform]](http://www.w3.org/ns/sosa/Platform) | [sosa:Platform](/docs/entities/sosa_platform) |
| `prov:` | [prov](http://www.w3.org/ns/prov#) | [:rdfterm[prov:Entity]](http://www.w3.org/ns/prov#Entity), [:rdfterm[prov:Activity]](http://www.w3.org/ns/prov#Activity), [:rdfterm[prov:Agent]](http://www.w3.org/ns/prov#Agent) | [prov:Activity](/docs/entities/prov_activity), [prov:Agent](/docs/entities/prov_agent) |
| `doco:` | [doco](http://purl.org/spar/doco/) | [`doco:Section`](http://purl.org/spar/doco/Section), [`doco:Paragraph`](http://purl.org/spar/doco/Paragraph) | N/A |
| `sh:` | [sh](http://www.w3.org/ns/shacl#) | [`sh:NodeShape`](http://www.w3.org/ns/shacl#NodeShape), [`sh:PropertyShape`](http://www.w3.org/ns/shacl#PropertyShape) | N/A |
| `skos:` | [skos](http://www.w3.org/2004/02/skos/core#) | [`skos:Concept`](http://www.w3.org/2004/02/skos/core#Concept), [`skos:Collection`](http://www.w3.org/2004/02/skos/core#Collection) | N/A |
